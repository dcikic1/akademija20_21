Mickey's Toontown is a themed land at Disneyland and Tokyo Disneyland, 
two theme parks operated by Walt Disney Parks & Resorts. 
At Tokyo Disneyland, this land is named Toontown. 
A similar land existed at the Magic Kingdom until 2011 and was named Mickey's Toontown Fair.
Walt Disney Studios Park in Disneyland Paris has a related land called Toon Studio.
